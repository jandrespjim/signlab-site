Frequently Asked Questions

Click to expand. If you don’t see your question, message us on WhatsApp—we reply fast.

What materials do you use?

We primarily use cold‑rolled steel from 1.5 to 3 mm thickness depending on the size, then apply a durable powder‑coated finish. Stainless steel and aluminum are available on request.

Can I request a custom size or font?

Absolutely. Send your wall measurements and reference photos. We’ll recommend dimensions and share a digital mockup for approval before cutting.

How do the signs mount?

Most pieces include pre‑drilled holes and optional stand‑off hardware. Some designs use hidden studs for a floating look. We provide a paper template for accurate placement.

Lead times and shipping?

Typical production is 5–7 business days after design approval. Shipping times vary by region; express options are available.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Packing is reinforced and corner‑protected to arrive safely.
